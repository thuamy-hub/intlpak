Our Story

Three Decades of
Coextrusion Craftsmanship

▶

Since 1991, Intlpak Enterprises Corporation has built its reputation one roll at a time as a dedicated multilayer coextrusion film manufacturer. Over thirty-five years, we've refined that technology into seven core product lines — from high-barrier EVOH film and retort-grade CPP to easy-peel and nylon thermoforming film — engineered for food, medical, cosmetics, electronics, and industrial customers worldwide.

Every line runs in a GMP clean-room environment, and every roll is backed by ISO 9001, ISO 22000, and HACCP certification — quality you can trace back to the raw material.

▶
Two Companies,
One Standard.

In 2017, we took that same discipline in a new direction. Our subsidiary, Green Intlpak, was founded to bring plant-fiber, fully biodegradable packaging to global foodservice and beverage brands — proof that sustainability and precision engineering aren't mutually exclusive.

Where Intlpak delivers the barrier performance food safety demands, Green Intlpak answers the call for lower-carbon, plastic-free alternatives. Together, we give customers a single group they can turn to — whichever side of the packaging equation they're solving for.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long has Intlpak been in business?

Since 1991. We're a Taiwan-based multilayer coextrusion film manufacturer with over three decades of production experience.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We export worldwide from Taiwan under standard Incoterms (FOB, CIF, etc.) — ask our team for a quote and lead time to your destination.

What industries and applications do you serve?

Food, medical device and pharmaceutical packaging, cosmetics, and sustainable packaging — from high-barrier MAP film to compostable molded-fiber containers, across seven specialized product lines.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ varies by film structure and gauge, typically starting around 500–1,000 kg per grade. Contact our team with your target application and we'll confirm the exact MOQ for that film.

Can I request a free sample before placing an order?

Yes. Use the contact form to tell us the product line and grade you're evaluating, and we'll arrange a sample roll for testing on your line.

What certifications does Intlpak hold?

All films are produced in a GMP clean-room environment and backed by ISO 9001, ISO 22000, and HACCP certification, with full traceability back to raw materials.

Do you offer custom film structures or gauges?

Yes. Our R&D team can tailor barrier level, thickness, and lamination structure to your product, forming depth, and packaging line — reach out with your specs.

How can I become a distributor or partner?

We're always open to new regional partnerships — visit our Join Us page or reach out through the contact form to start the conversation.
