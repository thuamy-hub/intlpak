2 Product Description

High-Barrier Thermoforming Film for Vacuum Packaging

F1127 (NLB #80) is a NY(PA)/EVOH/PE thermoforming film built around an EVOH barrier core, designed as the sealing film in continuous automatic forming vacuum packaging lines. Once laminated with PET or OPP, it forms a complete high oxygen-barrier bag structure.

At 80μm, F1127 delivers the formability, twist resistance, and pinhole resistance needed for reliable vacuum seals, along with high transparency and gloss so packed products present cleanly on shelf.

3 Highlights

Why F1127 Performs

High oxygen barrier — OTR of just 0.8 cc/m²·24hr (20°C, 65% RH) extends shelf life for vacuum-packed food.
Excellent formability — twist resistance without pinholes keeps seals intact through automatic vacuum-forming lines.
High transparency and gloss — keeps vacuum-packed meat and ready-to-eat products looking fresh and shelf-ready.
Forms a complete barrier bag when laminated — pairs with PET or OPP to build a high oxygen-barrier vacuum package.
Robust mechanical strength — 500/460 kg/cm² MD/TD break point stands up to continuous automatic vacuum-forming lines.
Purpose-built for ready-to-eat packaging — suited to dried meat, sliced ham, pastry, and beef jerky sealing lines.
4 Application

Where F1127 Is Used

  • 1 Sealing film for continuous automatic forming vacuum packaging
  • 2 Vacuum packaging film for room-temperature, refrigerated, and frozen food
  • 3 Vacuum, deoxygenation, and Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) of beef, chicken, pork, seafood, livestock, and agricultural products
  • 4 Packaging of ready-to-eat products such as dried meat, sliced ham, pastry, and beef jerky
