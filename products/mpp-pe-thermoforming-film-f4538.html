2 Product Description

A Vacuum Thermoforming Base Film Built for Medical Device Packaging

F4538 is the 80μm entry gauge in Intlpak's MPP/PE thermoforming film series, engineered as the forming (base) film for medical vacuum thermoforming packaging lines. It is produced by computer-controlled co-extrusion with automatic foreign-object detection and thickness control, under clean-room production standards suited to medical-grade packaging.

Designed with high formability and strong pinhole resistance at a shallow 10–15mm forming depth, F4538 packages injection syringes, gauze, cotton pads, suction tubes, and other disposable medical devices with the high transparency and high gloss needed for clear device inspection, while remaining compatible with E.O. (ethylene oxide) sterilization processes.

3 Highlights

Why F4538 Performs

E.O. sterilization compatible — the MPP/PE structure withstands ethylene oxide sterilization cycles without compromising seal integrity.
High formability, pinhole resistant — engineered for clean draws at a 10–15mm forming depth without pinholing around device edges.
High transparency, high gloss — lets clinicians and inspectors visually verify syringes, gauze, and cotton pads through the sealed pack.
Clean-room production standard — computer-controlled co-extrusion with automatic foreign-object detection and thickness control.
Built for medical vacuum thermoforming lines — purpose-designed as the base film for continuous medical-device packaging equipment.
Part of a full gauge family — F4538 (80μm, 10–15mm draw) pairs with the deeper-draw F4539 (120μm, 30mm draw) for a range of device profiles.
