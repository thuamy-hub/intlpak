2 Product Description

A Heavier-Gauge Retort Sealing Film for Added Forming Confidence

F2237 is a 150μm NY/RCPP thermoforming film, the deeper-draw sibling of Intlpak's F2236 grade in the same retort/sous-vide family. It is built to withstand high-temperature retort food sterilization and sous-vide cooking, in addition to standard vacuum packaging at room temperature, refrigerated, and frozen conditions. Used as the sealing (top) film on continuous automatic vacuum thermoforming lines, it supports a suggested forming depth of 30–50mm with the added margin of a heavier gauge.

Like F2236, this grade is engineered for the best formability and twist resistance without pinholes, paired with high transparency, high gloss, and good gas barrier properties — giving retort-processed and sous-vide proteins a clean, retail-ready appearance that survives the added stress of thermal sterilization.

3 Highlights

Why F2237 Performs

Built for high-temperature retort sterilization — the NY/RCPP structure holds up under thermal sterilization cycles that standard NL film is not designed for.
Heavier gauge than F2236 — the extra 15μm gives added strength margin for slightly more demanding retort or forming conditions.
Sous-vide ready — suitable for the long, precise, sealed cook cycles used in sous-vide packaging.
Works across the temperature range — one film for room-temperature, refrigerated, and frozen vacuum packaging, not just cold-chain use.
Twist- and pinhole-resistant — the best formability at this gauge, holding up through vacuum forming and thermal processing without pinholing.
Good gas barrier — supports vacuum shelf-life through storage and distribution after processing.
