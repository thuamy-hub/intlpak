2 Product Description

The Deepest-Draw Base Film in the Medical Nylon Series

F2606-M is the heaviest gauge in Intlpak's NE nylon/PE medical thermoforming film series at 150μm, engineered to support the deepest forming cavities in the family — 30 to 50mm — for large sterilized medical equipment that exceeds the capacity of the 80–120μm grades. It is produced by computer-controlled co-extrusion with automatic foreign-object detection and thickness control, under clean-room production standards.

Despite the heavier gauge required for deep-draw forming, F2606-M carries forward the series' formability and pinhole resistance, along with high transparency and high gloss for clear inspection, and remains compatible with both E.O. (ethylene oxide) and gamma (r-ray) sterilization processes.
