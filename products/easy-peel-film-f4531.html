2 Product Description

The Entry-Gauge Grade in Intlpak's Easy-Peel Lidding Series

F4531 is a heat-seal layer engineered for the food-contact side of high-temperature processed food packaging. After lamination, the film forms an easy-open lid material for PP food containers, suitable for pet food, canned food, microwaveable meals, and ready-to-eat food packaging.

As the lightest gauge in the family alongside F45A5 (50μm) and F45A7 (70μm), F4531 gives converters a lower-cost lidding option for applications that don't need the thicker gauges' extra stiffness, while still holding up to pressurized retort sterilization up to 130°C.

3 Highlights

Why Converters Choose F4531

Rated to 130°C — holds seal integrity through pressurized retort sterilization, the same heat rating as the thicker F45A5/F45A7 grades.
Lightest gauge in the series — a lower-cost entry point for lidding needs that don't require F45A5 or F45A7's extra thickness.
Airtight, easy-open — a clean, tear-free peel with a secure seal in between, keeping the pack airtight until opened.
Forms a complete lid after lamination — becomes a heat-seal layer for lid material once laminated to a barrier structure.
Suitable for PP containers — sized and formulated specifically for polypropylene retort food packaging.
Food-contact safe — suitable for pet food, canned food, microwaveable meals, and ready-to-eat food packaging.
