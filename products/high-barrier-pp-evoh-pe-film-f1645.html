2 Product Description

F1645 — High Barrier PP/EVOH/PE Film

F1645 is listed in Intlpak's official High Functional Film catalog (Ver. EN260327-CAT) as a 45μm grade within the High Barrier PP/EVOH/PE Film line — the same family as F1648-EPL, built around an EVOH barrier core for high oxygen-barrier performance in food, medical, and cosmetics packaging.this product line, it is built around an EVOH barrier core for high oxygen-barrier performance in food, medical, and cosmetics packaging.

3 Highlights

F1645 Product Line Highlights

High oxygen barrier — EVOH barrier core provides high OTR protection for flavor, freshness, and shelf life; same architecture as the rest of the PP/EVOH/PE line.
Eco-friendly construction — a lighter-footprint alternative to foil laminates without sacrificing barrier performance.
Same family as F1648-EPL — 45μm sibling in the High Barrier PP/EVOH/PE Film line; shares the same EVOH-core barrier technology.
Broad application range — EVOH barrier structure suits food, medical care, cosmetics, and hygienic product packaging across multiple industries.
Proven barrier technology — EVOH-core multilayer co-extrusion delivers reliable oxygen and moisture barrier performance in demanding packaging environments.

4 Application

Where F1645 Is Used

  • 1 Food, medical care, cosmetics, and hygienic cleaning product packaging
  • 2 Applications requiring high oxygen-barrier properties
  • 3 Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) of fresh proteins — beef, chicken, pork, and seafood
  • 4 Can be printed or laminated with PET, OPP, or NY film for finished flexible packaging structures
