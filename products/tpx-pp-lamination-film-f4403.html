2 Product Description

Dual-Ovenable Lamination Film for Sustainable Pulp Containers

F4403 is a TPX-PP co-extrusion lamination film built for pulp-molded (molded fiber) food containers, combining a plant-fiber base with a food-contact PP/TPX film layer. The result cuts overall plastic usage by up to 85% compared with a traditional solid-plastic tray, without giving up heat performance.

Where standard pulp containers are typically microwave-only, F4403's lamination upgrades the container to fully dual-ovenable — safe in both microwave and conventional oven — while its FDA-approved food-contact-grade TPX blend improves thermal stability and helps prevent plastic additives from migrating into food.

3 Highlights

Why F4403 Performs

Up to 85% less plastic — built on a renewable, molded plant-fiber base instead of a solid plastic tray.
Dual-ovenable — fully microwave- and oven-safe, breaking through the heating limits of standard pulp containers.
FDA-approved, anti-migration — food-contact-grade TPX co-extrusion blocks additive migration into food.
Superior oil and grease barrier — PP food-contact layer resists heat, moisture, and grease with minimal additives, no softening or leaking.
ESG-aligned — lowers corporate carbon footprint and helps brands meet plastic-reduction and sustainability goals.
Ready for retort applications — suitable for retort pouch inner packaging and post-sterilization foods, in addition to fresh and frozen meals.
