2 Product Description

Maximum-Barrier Coextrusion Film for Demanding Packs

F1256 (EVE #60) is a PE/EVOH/PE coextrusion film built around an EVOH core layer, engineered as the top thickness and top barrier grade in Intlpak's EVE family — giving the lowest oxygen transmission and highest mechanical strength of the series, alongside fragrance retention.

The film carries a corona treatment on one side, so it laminates cleanly with PET, OPP, or NY to build packaging structures with the moisture barrier, oxygen barrier, mechanical strength, and heat-sealing performance that food, medical, and cosmetics packaging lines need. Its heavier gauge trades a higher haze reading for the family's toughest yield and break strength, making it the choice for demanding MAP and industrial packaging.

3 Highlights

Why F1256 Performs

Lowest oxygen transmission in the family — OTR of just 0.65 cc/m²·24hr (20°C, 65% RH), the tightest barrier of all EVE grades.
Highest mechanical strength — yield point of 240–250 kg/cm² and tensile modulus up to 7400 kg/cm², the strongest grade in the series.
Fragrance and aroma retention — keeps volatile aromas sealed in for cosmetics and flavor-sensitive food products.
Corona-treated for lamination — bonds reliably with PET, OPP, or NY without added tie layers.
Built for demanding structures — the heaviest gauge in the family resists pinholing and flex-cracking on high-fill or bulky packs.
Broad application range — one film structure covers food, medical care, cosmetics, and hygienic product packaging.
4 Application

Where F1256 Is Used

  • 1 Food, medical care, cosmetics, and sanitary/hygienic cleaning product packaging
  • 2 Applications requiring the highest oxygen-barrier properties in the EVE range
  • 3 Can be printed or laminated with other film materials
  • 4 Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) of beef, chicken, pork, seafood, livestock, and agricultural products
