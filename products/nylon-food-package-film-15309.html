2 Product Description

The Deep-Draw Flagship of the NL Nylon/PE Series

15309 is the heaviest-gauge grade in Intlpak's NL nylon/PE thermoforming film family at 262μm — an NY/PE structure used as the sealing (top) film on continuous automatic vacuum thermoforming lines. Its heavier gauge supports a suggested forming depth of 60–100mm, the deepest draw range in the series, for bulky cuts and multi-portion packs that shallower NL grades cannot form.

Despite the extra thickness needed for deep-draw strength, 15309 keeps the same formability, twist resistance, and pinhole resistance the NL series is known for, paired with high transparency and high gloss for a clean, retail-ready appearance and a good gas barrier through the distribution chain.

3 Highlights

Why 15309 Performs

Deepest draw depth in the NL family — suggested forming depth of 60–100mm handles bulky cuts and multi-portion vacuum packs.
Highest tensile modulus in the series — 4000/4500 kg/cm² (MD/TD) at 2% elongation gives the rigidity needed for heavy, deep-formed loads.
Twist- and pinhole-resistant at heavy gauge — the best formability at 262μm, holding up through deep vacuum forming without pinholing.
Good gas barrier — supports vacuum shelf-life for refrigerated and frozen proteins, even at deep-draw volumes.
Built for high-speed lines — designed as a sealing film for continuous automatic vacuum thermoforming equipment.
Part of a full gauge family — the same NL series scales from 50μm sealing film up to 262μm for deep-draw forming (see full table below).
4 Application

Where 15309 Is Used

  • 1 Deep-draw vacuum packaging film for refrigerated and frozen food
  • 2 Bulky and multi-portion cuts of beef, chicken, pork, seafood, sausage, and fresh meat
  • 3 Ham, bacon, duck, and other ready-to-eat and processed meats requiring deep cavities
  • 4 Sealing film on continuous automatic vacuum thermoforming lines, 60–100mm forming depth
