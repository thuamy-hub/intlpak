2 Product Description

An Extra-Deep-Draw Gauge for the Bulkiest Cuts

F2647 is the 180μm grade in Intlpak's NL nylon/PE thermoforming film series, built for a 60–70mm forming depth — engineered for whole primal cuts, large bone-in joints, and multi-piece bulk packs that need the deepest cavity in the mid-family lineup. After lamination with PET or OPP film, it forms a heavy-duty vacuum bag that stretches into a deep mold without stress-whitening or pinholing.

F2647 delivers the family's highest elongation (620%/600% MD/TD) to accommodate the extreme stretch of a deep draw, while keeping a corona-treated NY surface for dependable lamination bonding and the NL series' signature gas barrier for extended vacuum shelf-life on large, high-value cuts.

3 Highlights

Why F2647 Performs

Extra-deep 60–70mm forming depth — the deepest cavity in the mid-family lineup, built for whole primal cuts and bulky bone-in items.
Highest elongation in the family — 620%/600% MD/TD elongation absorbs the extreme stretch of a deep draw without tearing.
High tensile modulus — 3400/3200 kg/cm² 2% modulus MD/TD keeps the deep-formed bag rigid enough to protect bulky contents in transit.
Corona-treated NY surface — dependable ink adhesion and lamination bonding for heavy-duty production runs.
Good gas barrier for extended shelf-life — supports the longer distribution windows large, high-value cuts often require.
Part of a full gauge family — the same NL series scales from 50μm sealing film up to 262μm for the very deepest forming (see full table below).
4 Application

Where F2647 Is Used

  • 1 Extra-deep-draw vacuum packaging film for refrigerated and frozen food, 60–70mm forming depth
  • 2 Whole primal cuts of beef and pork, and large bone-in poultry portions
  • 3 Whole ham, large bacon slabs, whole duck, and other bulky processed meats
  • 4 Large-format or multi-piece seafood and meat packs requiring maximum cavity volume
  • 5 Extra-deep-draw sealing/forming film on continuous automatic vacuum thermoforming lines
