2 Product Description

A Finished, Ready-to-Fill Container Built for Extreme Heat and Sustainability

The TPX-PP Laminated Molded Fiber Container is a finished foodservice container — not a raw film grade — built with a hybrid structure of roughly 90% molded fiber (paper base) and 10% TPX-PP laminated film, topped with a clear PET lid. It is engineered as a direct, high-performance replacement for conventional 100%-plastic rigid PP containers, cutting plastic content down to just 10.75% of a comparable all-plastic tray.

Despite its plant-fiber base, the container withstands fatty, high-heat foods up to 160°C and is fully dual-ovenable — safe in both microwave and conventional oven. A leak-proof, multi-compartment design, superior thermal insulation, and a non-stick surface make it equally suited to curries, bento meals, fried foods, and bakery items, while remaining FDA-compliant and SGS-certified for food-contact safety.
