2 Product Description

High-Clarity, High-Barrier Coextrusion Film

F1249 (EVE #40) is a PE/EVOH/PE coextrusion film built around an EVOH core layer for high oxygen-barrier performance, together with fragrance retention and good transparency — all in an environmentally considered structure with lower overall material use than foil laminates.

The film carries a corona treatment on one side, so it laminates cleanly with PET, OPP, or NY to build packaging structures with the moisture barrier, oxygen barrier, mechanical strength, and heat-sealing performance that food, medical, and cosmetics packaging lines need.

3 Highlights

Why F1249 Performs

High oxygen barrier — OTR of just 2.6 cc/m²·24hr (20°C, 65% RH) protects flavor, freshness, and shelf life.
Fragrance and aroma retention — keeps volatile aromas sealed in for cosmetics and flavor-sensitive food products.
High transparency — haze of only 4.8% keeps product presentation clean and shelf-ready.
Corona-treated for lamination — bonds reliably with PET, OPP, or NY without added tie layers.
Balanced mechanical strength — MD/TD tensile modulus and elongation suited to high-speed converting and lamination lines.
Broad application range — one film structure covers food, medical care, cosmetics, and hygienic product packaging.
4 Application

Where F1249 Is Used

  • 1 Food, medical care, cosmetics, and sanitary pad packaging
  • 2 Applications requiring high oxygen-barrier properties
  • 3 Can be printed or laminated with other film materials
  • 4 Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) of beef, chicken, pork, seafood, livestock, and agricultural products
