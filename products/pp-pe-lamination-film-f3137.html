2 Product Description

The Heavier-Gauge Grade in a New Lamination Film Line

F3137 is listed in Intlpak's official High Functional Film catalog (Ver. EN260327-CAT) as the 70μm grade in a new PP/PE Lamination Film line, positioned for disposable food containers that need grease and water resistance. It sits alongside F3131 (43μm) as the two gauges currently listed in this line.

3 Highlights

Why F3137 Is Specified

Grease resistant — engineered for disposable food-container applications where oil and grease contact is expected.
Water resistant — holds up against moisture and condensation in disposable container use.
Microwave-safe — PP/PE construction survives microwave heating without deformation or leaching.
Food-contact grade — safe PP/PE polymer construction throughout; suitable for direct food contact applications.
Heaviest gauge in the line — 70μm (F3137) for containers requiring maximum rigidity and structural strength; lighter 43μm (F3131) also available.
Disposable container optimized — designed for food service and takeout packaging formats requiring reliable grease and moisture protection.
4 Application

Where F3137 Is Used

  • 1 Heavy-duty disposable food containers requiring structural strength
  • 2 Microwave-ready meal trays where extra film rigidity is required
  • 3 Grease-intensive foods: fried items, curries, and oily dishes
  • 4 Bulk food service packaging where thicker, more robust lamination is specified
