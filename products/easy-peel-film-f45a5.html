2 Product Description

A Purpose-Built Easy-Open Lid for Retort Food

Easy Peel Film for Retort Food is a heat-seal layer engineered specifically for the food-contact side of high-temperature processed food packaging. Laminated into a structure such as PET 12μm // AL 9μm // ONY 15μm // EO-RCPP 50μm, it forms an easy-open lid material for PP containers that must survive pressurized retort sterilization at 121–130°C.

Unlike lidding films that lose seal integrity or shift in peel strength after autoclaving, F45A5 is formulated to hold a stable heat-sealing tension with only a small, predictable difference in peel force before and after sterilization — giving converters and co-packers a wide heat-sealing window of over 30°C to work with on the production line, while still opening cleanly by hand.

3 Highlights

Why Converters Choose F45A5

Rated to 130°C — holds seal integrity through commercial retort sterilization at 121–125°C, tested to 130°C.
30°C+ heat-sealing window — a wide sealing-temperature range keeps peel force consistent across production-line variation.
Stable before/after peel force — the peel-force curve barely shifts after 121°C / 30 min sterilization, so pack quality stays predictable.
Airtight, easy-open — a clean, tear-free peel with a secure seal in between, so the pack stays airtight until the customer opens it.
Proven laminate structure — PET 12μm // AL 9μm // ONY 15μm // EO-RCPP 50μm gives full aluminum-foil barrier plus easy-peel convenience.
Food-contact safe — suitable for pet food, canned food, microwaveable meals, and ready-to-eat food packaging.
