2 Product Description

A Purpose-Built Easy-Open Lid for Contact Lens Containers

Easy Peel Film for Contact Lens is a heat-seal layer engineered specifically for the product-contact side of contact lens blister packaging. Laminated into a structure such as PET 12μm // AL 9μm // ONY 15μm // EO-RCPP 35μm, it forms an easy-open lid material for PP contact lens containers that must survive pressurized high-temperature sterilization up to 130°C.

Because contact lens packaging demands a very controlled, low-force peel that a consumer can open with fingertips without splashing the saline solution inside, F4568 is formulated to hold a stable heat-sealing tension with only a small, predictable difference in peel force before and after sterilization — giving converters a wide heat-sealing window of over 30°C on the production line, while still opening cleanly and consistently by hand.
