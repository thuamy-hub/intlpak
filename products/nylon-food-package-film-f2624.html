2 Product Description

A Deeper-Draw Step Up from the F2646 Sealing Film

F2624 is the 80μm grade in Intlpak's NL nylon/PE thermoforming film series, built for a 10–15mm forming depth — a step up from the thin F2646 sealing film toward true vacuum-forming applications. After lamination with PET or OPP film, it forms a robust vacuum bag capable of holding its shape through the forming, sealing, and shipping process.

The added gauge gives F2624 more body for shallow-to-moderate draw cavities while retaining the same high transparency, high gloss, and pinhole-resistant formability that defines the NL series — keeping vacuum-packed proteins looking clean and retail-ready.

3 Highlights

Why F2624 Performs

Built for real vacuum forming — 80μm gives enough body for a 10–15mm draw depth without pinholing during the form.
High transparency, high gloss — low 5.7% haze keeps vacuum-packed meat and seafood looking retail-ready.
Good gas barrier — supports vacuum shelf-life for refrigerated and frozen proteins.
Higher break strength than F2646 — 460/470 kg/cm² MD/TD break point holds up to deeper cavity forming and handling.
Laminates for a full vacuum bag — combines with PET or OPP film to complete a vacuum-packaging structure.
Part of a full gauge family — the same NL series scales from 50μm sealing film up to 262μm for deep-draw forming (see full table below).
4 Application

Where F2624 Is Used

  • 1 Vacuum packaging film for refrigerated and frozen food, 10–15mm forming depth
  • 2 Fresh beef, chicken, pork, seafood, sausage, and fresh meat products
  • 3 Ham, bacon, duck, and other ready-to-eat and processed meats
  • 4 Portioned cuts and small-format proteins needing a shallow forming cavity
  • 5 Sealing/forming film on continuous automatic vacuum thermoforming lines
