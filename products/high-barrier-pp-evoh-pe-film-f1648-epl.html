2 Product Description

High-Clarity, High-Barrier Coextrusion Film

F1648-EPL (PVE #45) is a PP/EVOH/PE coextrusion film built around an EVOH barrier core, delivering high oxygen-barrier performance together with high transparency in an environmentally considered structure with lower overall material use than foil laminates.

The film carries a corona treatment on one side, so it laminates cleanly with PET, OPP, or NY to build packaging structures with the moisture barrier, oxygen barrier, mechanical strength, and heat-sealing performance that food, medical, and cosmetics packaging lines need.

3 Highlights

Why F1648-EPL Performs

High oxygen barrier — OTR of just 2.6 cc/m²·24hr (20°C, 65% RH) protects flavor, freshness, and shelf life.
High transparency — haze of only 8% keeps product presentation clean and shelf-ready.
Eco-friendly construction — a lighter-footprint alternative to foil laminates without sacrificing barrier performance.
Corona-treated for lamination — bonds reliably with PET, OPP, or NY without added tie layers.
High elongation, high strength — 600% MD/TD elongation with a 400 kg/cm² break point handles high-speed converting and lamination lines.
Broad application range — one film structure covers food, medical care, cosmetics, and hygienic product packaging.
4 Application

Where F1648-EPL Is Used

  • 1 Food, medical care, cosmetics, and hygienic cleaning product packaging
  • 2 Applications requiring high oxygen-barrier properties
  • 3 Can be printed or laminated with other film materials
  • 4 Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) of fresh beef, chicken, pork, and seafood products
